CortesMed Scheduler

CortesMed Scheduler is a social media automation tool designed for content creators in the health and medical education space. It enables creators to schedule and publish short-form videos (Reels, Shorts, TikToks) across multiple platforms automatically.

The tool integrates with TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook APIs to allow creators to manage their content pipeline efficiently from transcription to scheduling without manual intervention on each platform.

Features

  • Automatic video transcription and caption generation
  • Multi-platform scheduling (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook)
  • No manual upload required after initial setup
  • Designed for medical content creators and health educators
